Our Comprehensive Approach

Navigating NYC's competitive private and independent school admissions requires strategic planning, expert guidance, and a deep understanding of what each school values. We help families identify the right-fit schools and create compelling applications that showcase your child's unique strengths.

Step 1

School Selection & Research

Finding the right-fit school is crucial. We help you identify schools that align with your child's academic needs, learning style, interests, and family values.
  • Comprehensive assessment of student's academic profile and interests
  • Research and identification of appropriate schools in NYC
  • Guidance on school philosophies, cultures, and academic programs
  • Development of balanced school list (reach, match, safety)
  • Timeline planning for applications and deadlines
  • School visit strategies and what to look for

Outcome: Curated list of schools that match your child's needs and family priorities

Step 2

Testing Strategy & Preparation

Most NYC private schools require standardized testing (ISEE/SSAT). We create a testing plan that maximizes your child's performance.
  • Determination of which test best suits your child (ISEE vs. SSAT)
  • Optimal testing timeline and registration guidance
  • Connection with test prep resources and tutoring support
  • Test-taking strategy development
  • Score interpretation and school submission strategy

Outcome: Strategic testing plan and strong standardized test performance

Step 3

Application Development

Crafting compelling applications that authentically showcase your child's strengths, character, and potential contributions to the school community.
  • Parent and student statement brainstorming and development
  • Essay editing and refinement (maintaining authentic voice)
  • Application review for completeness and consistency
  • Recommendation letter strategy and coordination
  • Activity sheet and resume development
  • Supplemental materials guidance (portfolios, writing samples)

Outcome: Polished, compelling applications that stand out to admissions committees

Step 4

Interview Preparation & Final Steps

Preparing your family for interviews and ensuring successful completion of all admissions requirements.
  • Student interview coaching and practice sessions
  • Parent interview preparation and guidance
  • School-specific interview strategies
  • Thank you note writing and follow-up guidance
  • Waitlist and re-application strategies if needed
  • Final school selection consultation and enrollment support

Outcome: Confident, prepared families and successful admissions outcomes
