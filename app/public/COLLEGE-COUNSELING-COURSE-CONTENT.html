Unlike programs that treat college applications as checklists, we identify and develop student passions, creating personalized pathways that showcase what makes each student unique across their entire high school career.

Our 4Steps2Success® Program

A comprehensive four-year roadmap designed for rising 9th graders. We recognize that student interests evolve, so we develop specific plans year-over-year while maintaining your comprehensive four-year strategy.

9th Identify

Foundation Year: Identify

A strong start in 9th grade builds the foundation for high school success. We closely monitor student progress to ensure academic excellence from day one.

  • Extensive review of grades, teacher comments, and academic performance
  • Identify courses requiring additional support
  • Guidance on sophomore year course selection including AP courses
  • Assistance applying for summer internships and pre-college programs
  • Advice on creating or joining extracurricular activities
  • Development of leadership skills and self-advocacy
  • Time-management and independent study skill building

Included: Bi-monthly check-ins with quarterly parent meetings

10th Formulate

Planning Year: Formulate

Students begin standardized test preparation, getting ahead of junior year stress. We determine whether ACT or SAT best fits each student.

  • Comprehensive standardized testing plan (which exam, when, how to excel)
  • Detailed analysis of freshman and current year grades
  • Junior year course selection guidance including AP courses
  • Assistance finding and applying to summer internships and competitive programs
  • Creation of first college list reflecting academic and personal goals
  • Continued extracurricular development and leadership expansion
  • Development of competition opportunities in areas of interest

Included: Bi-monthly check-ins, testing strategy development, summer opportunity curation

11th Execute

Keystone Year: Execute

The crucial year for making final impacts on academic profiles. We pay particular attention to student motivation, mental health, and stress reduction strategies.

  • Comprehensive grade analysis and academic support identification
  • Senior year course selection including AP courses
  • Creation of refined college list matching academic and personal goals
  • College visit guidance and networking with admissions advisors
  • Letter of recommendation strategy and planning
  • Professional experience summary, resume, and cover letter development
  • Interview skills development and mock interview practice
  • Common App essay brainstorming, outlining, editing, and proofreading
  • Application materials and deadline management

Included: Bi-monthly check-ins, supplemental office hours, email and text support, essay development

12th Apply

Application Year: Apply

Students are ahead of peers, having completed testing and built strong profiles. Now they tell their academic story and demonstrate genuine interest.

  • Finalize college list and devise Early Decision/Early Action strategy
  • Financial aid application assistance and scholarship identification
  • Finalize all college essays, supplementals, and resume
  • Complete application submission timeline
  • College interview preparation
  • Common Application review ensuring flawless completion
  • Management of all application materials, timelines, and deadlines
  • Post-offer consultation to determine best-fit school

Included: Tailored support throughout senior year with backup expert review before submission

Additional Program Options

  • Freshman year course selection guidance
  • Individualized roadmap for developing interests
  • Leadership profile building strategies
  • Community service and volunteer opportunity planning
  • Self-advocacy and time-management skill development

Rising Seniors
