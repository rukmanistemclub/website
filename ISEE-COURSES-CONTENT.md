  Stat 2 Label: Trinity, Dalton, Brearley, Collegiate & more

**Hero Buttons:**

- Secondary Button: [inquiry_button shortcode]

### Course Description
```
The Lower ISEE (formerly Primary Level) is designed for students in grades 4 and 5 applying to competitive independent elementary schools. This comprehensive preparation program builds the foundational skills needed to excel on test day.

**About the Lower ISEE Test**

The Independent School Entrance Examination (ISEE) Lower Level evaluates students' capabilities in reading, mathematics, and analytical thinking appropriate for early learners. Developed by the Educational Records Bureau (ERB), the Lower ISEE is accepted by over 1,200 independent schools worldwide.

**Test Format:**
- **Reading Comprehension:** 25 questions in 25 minutes
- **Mathematics Achievement:** 30 questions in 30 minutes
- **Quantitative Reasoning:** 30 questions in 30 minutes
- **Essay (Unscored):** 1 prompt in 30 minutes - sent to schools to demonstrate writing ability

**Target Scores:**
Competitive independent schools typically look for scores in the 7-9 stanine range (77th-99th percentile). Elite schools often expect scores consistently in the 8-9 stanine across all sections.

**Testing Timing:**
Students may take the ISEE only once per testing season (Fall: August-November, Winter: December-March, Spring/Summer: April-July). Realistically, only Fall and early Winter testing are viable for most students, as application deadlines typically fall in early January.

**What We Cover:**
- Foundational reading comprehension strategies
- Elementary arithmetic operations and problem-solving
- Pattern recognition and quantitative reasoning
- Vocabulary building appropriate for grade level
- Essay writing structure and development
- Full-length practice tests under timed conditions
- Test-taking strategies specific to the Lower ISEE format

**Program Format:**
- Small group classes (maximum 8 students)
- Personalized 1-on-1 tutoring options
- Year-round enrollment with rolling start dates
- Summer intensive bootcamp options
- Both online and in-person instruction available
```

## Implementation Instructions

### To Create Each Course in WordPress:

1. **Navigate to:** Courses → Add New (or your custom post type admin)

2. **Fill in Basic Info:**
   - Title
   - Slug
   - Category

3. **Fill in ACF Hero Fields:**
   - Hero Title
   - Hero Subtitle
   - Hero Stats (if available in ACF)
   - Hero Buttons

4. **Add Course Description:**
   - Copy the description from above
   - Paste into the WordPress editor or ACF description field

5. **Add Common Sections:**
   - In the course template parts, the shortcodes will automatically render:
     - `[why_choose_isee]` - Already created in plugin
     - `[testimonials]` - Already exists in plugin
   - These will display automatically based on the course template

6. **Publish** each course

### Result:
You'll have 3 complete ISEE courses with:
- Professional hero sections
- Comprehensive test information
- Why Choose NYC STEM Club section (4-card grid with Digital ISEE Ready)
- Testimonials section
- Consistent branding and styling with existing SHSAT/SAT-ACT courses
